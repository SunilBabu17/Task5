Sun Temple

A 13th-century temple dedicated to the Hindu Sun God, known for its exquisite architecture and intricate carvings.

Chandrabhaga Beach

A beautiful and secluded beach, known for its clean waters and pristine surroundings.

Ramachandi Temple

A beautiful temple dedicated to the goddess Ramachandi, situated on a picturesque location on the banks of the river Kushabhadra.

Konark Museum

A museum dedicated to the history and culture of Konark, showcasing a wide range of artifacts and exhibits.

Konark Beach

A pristine and secluded beach, perfect for a quiet retreat or a romantic getaway.

Archaeological Survey of India Museum

A museum dedicated to the archaeological finds from the Konark Sun Temple and the surrounding areas.

Konark Natya Mandap

A cultural center dedicated to classical Indian dance, located near the Konark Sun Temple.

Puri Beach

A popular beach destination near Konark, known for its lively atmosphere and water sports activities.

Jagannath Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Lord Jagannath, located in the nearby town of Puri.

ASI Museum, Puri

A museum dedicated to the artifacts and history of the Jagannath Temple and its surrounding areas.
